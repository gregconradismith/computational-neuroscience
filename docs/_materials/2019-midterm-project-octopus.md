are focused on <strong>animal intelligence</strong> (neuroethology), <strong>artificial intelligence</strong> (cybernetics), consciousness, language, and neuro-culture.  Your book choice for the midterm project may be - but is not required to be - related to your final project.

<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>Please send me an email indicating the book you have selected.</strong></span>

Read your selection over Spring Break and prepare for in-class discussions that will occur during the two weeks following Spring Break.

You have flexibility with regard to the deliverable (see due date on course calendar).   The <strong>media</strong> may be written, video, podcast, whatever would work best and show your creativity.   The <strong>form </strong>and<strong> audience</strong> may be any one of the following
<ul>
	<li>A critical review of the book written by you, an apprentice scholar, at the level of your expertise.</li>
	<li>A reader response essay written by you, a neuroscience major, at the level of an educated layperson</li>
	<li>A pedagogical summary written by you, an educational consultant, at the level of a high school student who you are trying to interest in neuroscience.</li>
</ul>
