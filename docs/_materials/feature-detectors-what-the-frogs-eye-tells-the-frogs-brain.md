<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Perceptron</h2>


<blockquote class="wp-block-quote">
<p>Inspired by the way neurons work together in the brain, <strong>the perceptron is a single-layer neural network</strong> – an algorithm that classifies input into two possible categories. The neural network makes a prediction – say, right or left; or dog or cat – and if it’s wrong, tweaks itself to make a more informed prediction next time. It becomes more accurate over thousands or millions of iterations.</p>
<cite>Lefkowitz 2019 (see Further Reading)</cite></blockquote>
