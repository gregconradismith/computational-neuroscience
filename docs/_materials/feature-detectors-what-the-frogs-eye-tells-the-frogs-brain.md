<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Perceptron</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Inspired by the way neurons work together in the brain,&nbsp;<strong>the perceptron is a single-layer neural network</strong>&nbsp;–&nbsp;an algorithm that classifies input into two possible categories.&nbsp;The neural network makes a prediction&nbsp;–&nbsp;say,&nbsp;right or left;&nbsp;or dog or cat&nbsp;–&nbsp;and if it’s wrong,&nbsp;tweaks itself to make a more informed prediction next time.&nbsp;It becomes more accurate over thousands or millions of iterations.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --><cite>Lefkowitz 2019 (see Further Reading)</cite></blockquote>
