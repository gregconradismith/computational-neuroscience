<hr />

The deliverable (and audience) was to be one of the following:
<ul>
	<li>Critical review for a neuroscience major</li>
	<li>Reader response essay for a science or non-science college graduate</li>
	<li>Pedagogical summary for an elementary, middle or high school student who might be interested in neuroscience</li>
</ul>
The media was to be chosen from written, video, or podcast.
